COMBAT

All attacks are GUARANTEED hits. Damage degree determines severity — zero damage can be narrated as evasion.
  1. Roll initiative (count minimum adjustments to succeed).
  2. State attack: target + targeted limb.
  3. Narrator declares consequence of success/failure.
  4. Decide whether to proceed.
  5. Spend ion → Roll Wound Die → declare wound value.
  6. Narrator applies reactions.
